who we are

The Technological Institute of the Philippines - Nurture Innovation Technology Revolution Office (T.I.P. NITRO) is a DOST funded university technology business incubator that supports clean and deep technology innovations. It is a hub where technological ideas have the opportunity to be bolstered and optimized to be ready for commercialization.

In T.I.P. NITRO, we believe that your IDEAS have the ability to CREATE IMPACT to our world.

And TOGETHER, we shall turn them into REALITY!
